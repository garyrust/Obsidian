Frequently Asked Questions

What's the real-world range I can expect?

Typical range varies from 40-80km depending on your riding style. You'll get maximum range when cruising at moderate speeds with pedal assist. Aggressive riding at top speeds (80-90 km/h) will reduce range significantly. Factors like rider weight, terrain, and temperature all affect actual performance.

How long does installation really take?

While we advertise a 50-minute installation time, most experienced riders complete it in 1-2 hours. We provide comprehensive tools and step-by-step video guides. First-time installers may want to schedule 2-3 hours or seek professional help for optimal setup.

About battery lifespan and charging

Using premium LG/Samsung cells, expect 800+ charge cycles over a 3-year lifespan with proper care. A full charge takes 4 hours 35 minutes. The intelligent BMS prevents overcharging and monitors cell health. The battery detaches easily for indoor charging.

How fast can it really go?

The system is capable of 80-90 km/h on flat terrain. However, real-world speeds depend heavily on conditions, rider weight, and battery charge level. The 110Nm torque allows for impressive acceleration and hill-climbing ability. Remember: this kit is designed for off-road use only.

What about noise and reliability?

The brushless direct drive motor operates quietly compared to geared systems. Military-grade construction and waterproof design ensure durability. Most users report thousands of kilometers without issues when properly maintained.

Customization & Control Features

The 3.5" TFT display allows full customization of power delivery, speed limits, and pedal assist levels. Intelligent cruise control maintains steady speeds, while the 6km/h walk assist helps in tight spaces. The controller can be fine-tuned for your riding style.

Safety & Legal Considerations

This high-performance kit is designed for off-road use. We require torque arm installation and recommend professional setup for optimal safety. Users should be aware of local regulations regarding e-bike power limits and usage areas.

What maintenance is required?

Regular maintenance includes checking motor connections, ensuring battery contacts are clean, and inspecting the torque arm. The controller and battery systems are sealed and maintenance-free. We recommend periodic firmware updates for optimal performance.
