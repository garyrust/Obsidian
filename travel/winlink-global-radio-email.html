VARA Products, including VARA HF, VARA FM, VARA Chat, VARA Sat, and VARA Terminal, are now hosted on the Winlink Servers for distribution. Pactor 4 Becomes Legal January 8

January 7, 2024 -- Happy New Year!

RMS Gateway Sysops within the USA and it's Territories are urged to configure Pactor 4 if you are equipped to do so. At midnight in your US timezone, Monday, January 8 the FCC rule change removing digital mode symbol rate limits on amateur radio HF bands becomes effective. Pactor 4 becomes a legal mode in the USA.

--Winlink Development Team

US Symbol Rate Rule Change and Pactor 4 Use

clippingsDecember 7, 2023--Today the final report and order of the FCC removing the symbol rate limits on amateur HF bands was published in the Federal Register.
Read it here.

Within the US and it's territories, the transmission of Pactor 4 signals on the bands becomes legal on January 8, 2024, 30 days after today's publication date. Network administrators will continue to spot violators on the Winlink system until January 8. Please observe the rules and don't jump the gun.

--The Winlink Team
